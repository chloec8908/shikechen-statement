Artist Statement


I create from emotions.

People use writing to record their diaries; I prefer my art to alternate words. My current stage of making art all comes from a sudden moment of an idea that comes to my mind. It could be from anything else; the daily and surroundings of my life. I took music as one of the importance of my influence in creation. Inspirations occasionally come from a song that represents the mood of my day. I would use this sense of emotions into building my art visually. I began to cooperate my art with music together and bring vocal to be visual to help my audiences visualize their emotions through this piece of music. I’ll use my ability to convey my drawing ideas by collecting all the feelings and piecing them into my artwork. It comes to a struggle sometimes when a piece is about an experience that I never have before, and I would have to interpret it in a way from my perspective of my own life experience.
